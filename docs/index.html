Welcome

Thanks for joining us on this interactive journey into global terrorism. Our platform offers a comprehensive look at terrorism patterns from 1970 to 2020. To make the most of your experience, here's a quick guide to help you navigate:

📊 Group Profiles

Begin your exploration by diving into data on the ten deadliest terrorist organizations. Witness the fatalities and attack methods through our detailed bar chart and heatmap. Every visual is interactive – hover over the elements for more details.

📍 Attack Hotspots

Ever wondered how terrorism activities are distributed globally over time? Our interactive tool lets you examine the worldwide spread of terrorist attacks for any chosen year. Use the slider to 'time-travel', and the map to get detailed info about each attack.

🕰️ Temporal Patterns

Discover the frequency of attacks across different days and months with our intuitive heatmap. Each cell represents the total number of attacks on a specific day across all years. The darker the color, the higher the attack count.

🌍 Regional Impact

Zoom into specific geographical regions to scrutinize terrorism trends. Choose a region, watch it highlight on the map, and observe the scatterplot of fatal attacks. Every point on the scatterplot tells a story – hover over to uncover it.

🗺️ Country-Specific Analysis

Choose a country from the dropdown menu to view its specific terrorism patterns. As you select a country, watch the map highlight and a streamgraph unveil the frequency of attacks over the years. The streamgraph is interactive too – explore to find out more.

We're here to assist! If you need further help or have any questions, please consult our guidebook or feel free to contact us.

Welcome to 'Unveiling Patterns: A Data-Driven Insight into Global Terrorism'

Our platform brings you an interactive exploration of data sourced from the Global Terrorism Database (GTD). We delve into the depths of half a century, from 1970 to 2020, but please note that the year 1993 is missing from the GTD.

🔍 Our Aim

Our mission is to present an unbiased, comprehensive view of global terrorism patterns. We've focused on diverse facets, including attack frequency, casualties, seasonality, regional trends, and methods and targets of attacks.

🎨 Interactive Visualizations

We've carefully designed various interactive visualizations, each honed to highlight specific aspects of the data. From an overview of terrorist groups and their preferred methods of attack, to a deep-dive into country-specific trends, we invite you to navigate these visuals and unearth the hidden patterns of global terrorism.

🌐 Global Impact

By presenting this data, our goal is to foster a global understanding of the patterns and impacts of terrorism. We must mention the sensitive nature of this topic, and we urge you to handle the information with care.

🎯 Relevance

Grasping the patterns of terrorism is vital for everyone – from policymakers and security agencies, to researchers and everyday citizens. We hope this platform aids in comprehending the scope of this global issue, identifying hotspots, discerning trends, and potentially, inferring underlying causes and implications.

📚 Our Objective

The goal of this project is to offer an accessible tool for understanding the landscape of global terrorism. By doing so, we aim to contribute to informed discussions, policy making, and educational endeavors. We believe in data neutrality, providing an unbiased view while leaving room for interpretation based on the data.

🚀 Ready to Begin?

Feel free to embark on your journey by selecting any of the five categories. If you need assistance navigating the website, our 'Help' button on the top right corner is your guide. Let's start uncovering patterns together.
